Automation — Barbara's Domain

n8n Workflows to Build

Five workflows handle the full attendee lifecycle. All use tools Barbara already has. Google Forms replaces any paid Zoom webhook.

Workflow 1 — Barbara builds
Registration Capture & Welcome
Google Sheets trigger fires when a new form response arrives. Upserts record to Airtable, sends Gmail confirmation with YouTube Live link and Joy's bio, fires optional Twilio SMS.
Google Sheets Trigger→ Airtable Upsert→ Gmail Confirm→ IF: has cell?→ Twilio SMS
Workflow 2 — Barbara builds
Reminder Sequence (Per Webinar)
Scheduled at 7 days, 1 day, and 1 hour before each session. Pulls registered Airtable records, sends reminder by channel. 1-hour reminder is SMS only for maximum open rate.
Schedule (7d)→ Airtable Filter→ Gmail | Schedule (1hr)→ Twilio SMS
Workflow 3 — Barbara triggers manually
Post-Webinar Follow-Up & Upsell Sequence
7-day drip: Day 0 replay + Joy's resource PDF. Day 2 value email from Joy. Day 3 soft upsell. Day 5 CTA. Day 7 last chance. Updates Airtable status at each step.
Manual Trigger→ Wait Nodes (×5)→ Gmail Sequence→ Airtable Update
Workflow 4 — Barbara triggers manually
No-Show Re-Engagement
Barbara updates Airtable attendance from YouTube analytics after each session. Workflow pulls no-show records and sends "We missed you" email with replay link + Joy's key takeaway.
Manual Trigger→ Airtable: Attended=False→ Gmail Replay→ Tag: No-Show
Workflow 5 — After Webinar 5
Series Completion — VIP Upsell
Pulls all records who attended 3+ sessions. Sends VIP celebration email from Barbara + Joy with exclusive early-bird paid offer. Tags them as VIP in Airtable for all future campaigns.
Post-W5 Trigger→ Airtable: ≥3 attended→ VIP Email (B + J)→ Tag: VIP Segment
Joy's role in automation: Joy provides the content for all emails — resource PDFs, follow-up tips, and the upsell pitch. Barbara builds the workflows and presses go. Clean handoff between Finance and Tech.
